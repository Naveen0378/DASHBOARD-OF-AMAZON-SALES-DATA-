# Amazon Sales Analysis and Interactive Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **Amazon sales data** using data analytics and business intelligence techniques. An **interactive dashboard** is developed to visualize sales performance, revenue trends, customer regions, product categories, ratings, and payment methods.  
The dashboard helps stakeholders make **data-driven decisions** in the e-commerce domain.

## 📖 Abstract
The rapid growth of e-commerce platforms has resulted in massive volumes of transactional data. Amazon, being one of the world’s largest e-commerce companies, relies on analytical insights to understand customer behavior and revenue patterns.  
This project focuses on analyzing Amazon sales data and presenting insights through an interactive dashboard highlighting key performance indicators (KPIs) such as **Total Revenue, Quantity Sold, Ratings, Regional Performance, and Payment Method Distribution**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze Amazon sales data using data analytics techniques  
- Calculate and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs)  
- Study regional and product category-wise sales performance  
- Analyze customer purchasing and payment behavior  
- Design an interactive and user-friendly sales dashboard  

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

### Dataset Summary
The dataset consists of Amazon sales transaction records collected across multiple regions and time periods. Each record represents a customer order.

### Dataset Attributes
- **Order Date** – Date of purchase  
- **Product Category** – Beauty, Books, Electronics, Fashion, Sports, Home & Kitchen  
- **Price** – Product price  
- **Quantity Sold** – Number of units sold  
- **Rating** – Customer rating  
- **Customer Region** – Asia, Europe, Middle East, North America  
- **Payment Method** – Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Wallet, Cash on Delivery  
- **Revenue** – Total revenue per order  

---

## 🔧 Data Preprocessing
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing values  
- Formatted date fields  
- Created calculated measures (Total Revenue, Average Rating)  
- Standardized region and payment method categories

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
- North America and Asia generate the highest revenue  
- Beauty and Books contribute significantly to total revenue  
- Digital payments (UPI, Wallet) show high adoption  
- Higher quantity sold results in higher customer reviews  
- Sales remain stable across most days with minor fluctuations
